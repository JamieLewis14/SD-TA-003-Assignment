What is the CDL?

The Call of Duty League is a professional esports competition featuring the world‚Äôs top Call of Duty players and organizations. Structured around high-level seasonal play, the CDL brings together global teams who compete across a series of matches, tournaments, and championship events. The league showcases elite gameplay, strategic depth, and evolving metas that define competitive Call of Duty.

Built on a franchised team model, the CDL emphasizes consistent competitive standards, broadcast quality, and fan engagement. Each season highlights rivalries, standout performers, and major storylines as teams push toward qualification for the year-ending championship.

This section gives visitors a simple overview of what the CDL represents, why it exists, and how it shapes the competitive landscape of Call of Duty.
